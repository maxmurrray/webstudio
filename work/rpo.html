Ready Paper One

A resume builder so simple and free that it doesn't even look like one.

Free Tool Product Design Minimal
Every resume builder is more complicated than it needs to be. We fixed that.
Resume builders are bloated. They're paywalled. They make your resume look exactly like everyone else's who paid for the same plan. Ready Paper One is the opposite — completely free, radically simple, and the output looks like you made it yourself at home on a Sunday afternoon. That's the point.
The Problem

Everyone can tell you used a resume builder.

The telltale signs are everywhere: the two-column layout with a colored sidebar, the icon set that ships with every template, the watermark on the free version, the font that every Canva resume uses. Recruiters see 200 of these a week.

A resume that looks like you typed it in Google Docs — or better, that you know your way around design — stands out immediately. That's what Ready Paper One produces without any extra effort from the user.

The Build

Fill it out. Download it. Done.

No account. No email. No credit card. You open the app, fill in your information across a few clean sections, and download a polished PDF. The interface gets out of the way entirely — it's just fields and a live preview.

The output uses real typographic hierarchy: correct leading, proper spacing between sections, a layout that a trained designer would actually produce. It doesn't scream "I used an app."

Key Features
Completely Free
No freemium. No watermarks. No "upgrade to download." The whole product is free because a resume builder shouldn't cost money.
No Account Required
Open it, fill it out, download it. Your data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere.
Looks Hand-Crafted
The output looks like you made it yourself — clean, typographically correct, and free from the template aesthetic that tags every other builder.
Instant PDF
One-click PDF export. No email, no cloud save required. The file is yours immediately.
