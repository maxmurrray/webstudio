Nōri Sushi

A sushi restaurant site that matches the food: clean, precise, and worth the wait.

Restaurant Web Design Mobile-First
A dining experience that starts before you walk in the door.
Nōri Sushi needed a web presence as sharp as their rolls. We built a clean, minimal restaurant site focused on atmosphere, menu, and conversion — getting people to reserve a table. No clutter, no stock photography, no template energy.
The Design

Minimal by choice, not by budget.

The visual language of Nōri pulls from Japanese design principles — negative space, asymmetric balance, a strict grid. The color palette is intentionally restrained: near-white, ink black, and a single warm accent. The typography is set with precision at every breakpoint.

Photography is hero-forward. The first thing you see is food that makes you hungry. The second thing you do is book a table. That's the whole funnel.

Key Features
Interactive Menu
A scannable digital menu with category tabs. Each section loads instantly — no page refreshes, no PDF downloads.
Reservation Flow
Reservation CTA is present on every scroll depth. Integrated with a booking system so confirmations are automatic.
Mobile-First Build
Most restaurant searches happen on phones. Every layout decision was made mobile-first, desktop second.
Brand Identity
Logo, wordmark, color system, and typography — all original. The macron over the ō is intentional: it respects the Japanese language behind the name.
