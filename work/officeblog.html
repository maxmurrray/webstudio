Office Blog

Click a product. Get a blog. A platform that turns any storefront into a content machine.

Branding Platform Content
Every product has a story. Office Blog tells it automatically.
Office Blog is a content platform with a simple idea: connect your store, click any product, and get a fully branded blog post written in your brand's voice. We built the brand from nothing — name, logo, typography, color system — and designed the product around radical simplicity.
The Concept

Content marketing shouldn't require a content team.

Most e-commerce brands know they should have a blog. Almost none of them do — because writing takes time, costs money, and pulls founders away from running the business. Office Blog removes that friction entirely.

You click a product in your catalog. Office Blog reads the product name, description, and category, then generates a blog post that matches your brand voice and actually sounds human. No prompts to write. No editing required unless you want to.

Brand Identity

Built from scratch.

The name, logo, color palette, and typography were all created original. The visual identity leans editorial — clean, structured, confident. The logo mark is minimal and works at any size, from a browser favicon to a billboard. Type is set in a geometric sans that reads fast and feels modern without being trendy.

Color system: a warm off-white primary with a strong ink-black for type and a single accent color that carries all interactive elements. Nothing extra. Nothing decorative for its own sake.
