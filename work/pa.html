The Build

A landing page with one job: capture phone numbers.

The site is pure black. No distractions. A pair of animated SVG eyes stare back at you, following your cursor across the screen. Below them, a single phone input powered by Emotive — a top-tier SMS marketing platform used by major streetwear brands.

The moment someone submits their number, they get a welcome text automatically. That list becomes the audience for every future drop announcement, discount, and moment of chaos Mike & Henry cook up.

The Animations

Eyes that follow you everywhere.

The hero visual is a pair of SVG eyes that track cursor movement across the entire viewport. Each pupil calculates the angle from eye center to cursor position using trigonometry, then applies a bounded translate so the pupil stays inside the iris no matter where the mouse goes.

The intro animation staggers in the eyes, wordmark, and form on load. There's also an easter egg — click the right place and something unexpected happens.

Key Features
Emotive SMS Integration
Native iframe embed replaces a custom form. Every signup flows directly into their Emotive subscriber list and triggers an automated welcome text.
Animated SVG Eyes
Three eyes — two small, one large — track the cursor in real time. Pupils are bounded inside the iris, animating smoothly at 60fps.
Pure Black Aesthetic
No colors, no gradients, no images. Just black, white, and the brand. Mysterious by design — visitors don't know what they're signing up for yet.
Mobile-First
Responsive layout with 100dvh viewport fix for iOS Safari. The phone form is touch-optimized and opens the native keyboard on tap.
